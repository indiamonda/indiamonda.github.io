Custom music for Backrooms (replaces main menu and tutorial BGM)

Add these two files here:
  - menu.mp3    → plays on main menu (and when you press 1)
  - tutorial.mp3 → plays during tutorial (press 2 to switch)

In-game music is unchanged. Press 3 to unmute the game and stop custom music.

Supported formats: MP3 is most reliable in browsers.
