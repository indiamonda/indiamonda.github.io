HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE
Jump from platform to platform and travel as far as possible! Collect gems to increase your score. Avoid spikes and falling into the void.
CONTROLS
  • Jump: Press W, Arrow Up, Space, or click/tap anywhere
  • Double Jump: Jump again while in the air (2 jumps total)
  • Drop: Press S or Arrow Down to drop quickly through platforms
  • Mobile Drop: Tap and drag down to drop
GAME MECHANICS
  • You start with 2 jumps - use them wisely!
  • Landing on a platform restores your jumps
  • The game speeds up as you climb higher
  • Platforms become more spaced out at furthur distance
  • Gems give you points - collect as many as you can!
